OUR BEGINNING

The Story of 12Twelve

12TWELVE PTY (LTD) was born on December 1st, 2021, from the vision of Xitshembiso, who was born on the 12th of December. The name "12TWELVE" represents not just a date, but a personal connection to beauty, creativity, and dedication.

Located in the serene Willow Park Manor in Pretoria East, our salon has become a sanctuary where clients experience professional beauty services in a welcoming, peaceful environment. Every service is delivered with care, attention to detail, and a passion for making each guest feel special.
