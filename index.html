ABOUT US

Your Beauty Destination

12TWELVE PTY (LTD) is a gel nail salon located in Willow Park Manor, Pretoria East. Since its establishment on December 1st, 2021, the salon has been dedicated to offering a range of beauty services in a peaceful, welcoming space. Founded by Xitshembiso in December of 2021, each visit is crafted with care and attention.

While it currently has one location, there are exciting plans for more to come in the future.

Xitshembiso Shibiti

CEO & Founder | Gel Overlay & Extension Nail-Tech

About Me

My name is Xitshembiso Shibiti, the CEO & Founder of 12TWELVE PTY (LTD). I established the salon on December 1st, 2021, driven by a deep passion for nail artistry, self-care, and creating a space where clients feel confident, relaxed, and valued.

With professional training in gel overlays and nail extensions, I pride myself on precision, hygiene, and creativity. My mission is to deliver high-quality beauty services while building long-lasting relationships with clients through trust, consistency, and exceptional service.

Gavaza

Senior Beauty Therapist

About Me

Gavaza is a skilled and dedicated beauty therapist with a strong focus on relaxation, wellness, and client comfort. With hands-on experience in professional beauty treatments, she ensures every client receives personalized care tailored to their needs.

Known for her calm approach and attention to detail, Gavaza plays an essential role in maintaining the salon’s high service standards. Her goal is to help clients unwind, rejuvenate, and leave feeling refreshed, confident, and well cared for.
